Apex Legends is a battle royale shooter game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. It was released in February 2019. Before each match players select from pre-designed characters with distinctive abilities, known as "Legends". The game has two gameplay modes.

Developer Respawn Entertainment had previously developed Titanfall and its sequel Titanfall 2, both of which were published by Electronic Arts (EA) who eventually acquired Respawn Entertainment in 2017.

Battle Royale

20 three-person squads or 30 two-person duos land on an island and search for weapons and supplies before attempting to defeat all other players in combat. The available play area on the island shrinks over time, forcing players to keep moving or else find themselves outside the play area which can be fatal. The final team alive wins the round.

In Game Items

Apex Packs are loot boxes that provide random cosmetic items. You get them for free regular or you can buy them for real money. There are three types of Apex Packs:

Regular Apex Packs: they come in Rare, Epic, and Legendary rarities, and guarantee at least one item of the same or better rarity.

Collection Event Apex Packs: they always come in Epic rarity, and guarantee one item from the Collection Event it was obtained from.

Thematic Apex Packs: they always come in Rare rarity, and are split into two subtypes. Legend Thematic Packs: they guarantee one Rare or better item that hasn't been found yet for the Legend they are tied to. Event Thematic Packs: they guarantee one Rare or better item that hasn't been found yet for the Thematic Event they are tied to.

Ther is also a in game store where you can buy skins and other cosmetics for real money.

Ranked Apex

Apex Legends has its own dedicated ranked mode, which challenges you to git good with your favourite legend, rather than just mess around with their ultimates.
